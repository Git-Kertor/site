Portfolio (2020 - 2021)

I've compiled my Unity projects here, showcasing the progress I've made in game development over the years. Some of these were created with little to no prior experience, so the quality varies. Despite that, I'm committed to learning and continuously striving to create better and more polished games.

Joker Poker Simulator (2021)

Get dealt a hand of five cards and choose which ones to keep and which to replace. Earn rewards based on specific card combinations at the end of each round. Strategize carefully to maximize your chances of achieving high-value hands.

The Ether Orb (2021)

Space Shooter

Survive waves of enemies for a set time using an arsenal of weapons at your disposal. Destroy asteroids to earn extra bonuses and prepare for the ultimate challenge: defeating the final boss that appears when the countdown ends.

Download

Stabilize (2021)

Microgame

Balance a bouncing ball on a platform that gradually shrinks, tilting it left and right to keep the ball from falling off. The timer continuously counts up, as the game has no end. The challenge lies in maintaining control as the platform becomes increasingly difficult to navigate.

Download

CloneQube (2021)

Puzzle Game

A remake of the PS2 game Intelligent Qube. You play as a humanoid character stranded on a floating platform, facing waves of tumbling cubes. The objective is to destroy all non-dark cubes before they reach the platform's edge and fall off.

Download

Slidestep (2021)

Puzzle Game

Levels feature a grid with colored squares. Swiping in a specific direction moves the squares in that direction or another, depending on the color. The objective is to move all colored squares into the goal using as few swipes as possible.

Download

Swift Circles (2020)

Rhythm Game

Very basic tapping game. Numbered circles appear in sequence, ranging from one to nine. There are two special types of circles: pairs of circles with the same number, and circles with an arrow indicating the direction the player should swipe.

Download

TrineAxle (2020)

Rhythm Game

Notes approach from three different lanes that are further divided into two sides. The player must rotate toward the correct lane and choose the correct side to block the incoming note. There are single notes and double notes. Double notes must be blocked by switching into double mode. The gameplay is unique but hard to approach.
